My Story

I embarked on my journey of self discovery not long after the age of 2. That's when I was aware of first identity - a brother to my newborn sister. Since then, I became cognizant of the fact that I have many more identities within, it was just a matter of me unravelling those layers. I loved trying new things because it was novel and different from what I usually do and piqued my curiosity. And in doing so, it helped me realize things that I liked and didn't like, and differentiate between right and wrong.
If I never shadowed physicians, I would've never realized that I loved the Doctor-Patient interaction so much. If I never competed in robotics in the 5th grade, I would've never realized that I enjoyed the ways technology can help society in individuals' day-to-day lives.
